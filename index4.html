Recent courses

Baking Production

This short course immerses participants in the world of artisan bread and pastry making, focusing on hands-on experience and foundational techniques. Students will learn the art of crafting delicious bread and pastries while mastering the use of specialized tools and equipment.

Event Organizing

The purpose of this course is to enable the studentsto acquire general knowledge about "Event Management " and to become familiar with mangement techniques and strategies required for successful planning, promotion, implementation, and evaluation of special events.

Food And Beverage Operation

This comprehensive short course introduces participants to the essential skills and knowledge required for successful food and beverage service. Through a blend of theoretical and hands-on learning, students will develope proficiency in serving, table setting, and customer interaction, while mastering the use of specialized tools and equipment.

Financial Record Keeping

This course is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in bookkeeping. Whether you're starting a career in accounting or seeking to enhance your financial management abilities, this program will provide you with a solid foundation in bookkeeping.

Food Transformation

In this course the students will be equipped with a comprehensive understanding of various food processing techniques, safety considerations, and emerging trends, enabling them to make informed decisions in the food industry and contribute to developing innovative and safe food products.
